A strong, unique password is your first line of defense against hackers and data breaches. Our Password Generator creates cryptographically secure random passwords that are virtually impossible to crack.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long should my password be?

At minimum 12 characters, but 16+ is recommended. Each additional character exponentially increases the time needed to crack the password. Our generator supports passwords of any length.

Are generated passwords stored anywhere?

No! Passwords are generated entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Nothing is sent to any server, stored, or logged. Once you close the page, the password exists only where you've saved it.

Should I include special characters?

Yes! Including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, AND special characters makes your password significantly harder to crack. A 12-character password with all character types has over 475 trillion possible combinations.

How often should I change my passwords?

Modern security guidance says to change passwords only when there's a suspected breach, not on a fixed schedule. Using unique, strong passwords with a password manager is more important than frequent changes.
