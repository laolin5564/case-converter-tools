Frequently Asked Questions

How do I generate a strong secure password?

Set your desired length (16+ characters recommended), select character types (uppercase, lowercase, numbers, symbols), and click generate. The tool creates a cryptographically random password that's virtually impossible to crack.

What makes a password secure and strong?

A strong password is at least 12-16 characters long, uses a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special symbols, avoids dictionary words and personal information, and is unique for each account.

Is it safe to generate passwords online?

Yes! Our password generator runs entirely in your browser using the Web Crypto API. No passwords are ever sent to any server or stored anywhere. The generation happens locally on your device for complete privacy and security.
