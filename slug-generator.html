What is Slug Generator?

A slug generator converts any text into a clean, URL-friendly slug. Slugs are the URL-safe versions of titles used in web addresses — like 'my-blog-post-title' instead of 'My Blog Post Title!'.

Our Slug Generator handles spaces, special characters, accented letters, and Unicode text, converting them into lowercase, hyphen-separated URL slugs that are SEO-friendly and readable.

Every web developer, blogger, and CMS user needs clean URL slugs. They improve SEO, look professional, and make URLs easier to share and remember.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a URL slug?

A URL slug is the URL-friendly version of a page title. For example, a blog post titled 'Top 10 JavaScript Tips!' would have the slug 'top-10-javascript-tips'.

How are special characters handled?

Special characters like !@#$%^&* are removed. Accented characters (é, ü, ñ) are transliterated to their ASCII equivalents. Spaces become hyphens.

Why are slugs important for SEO?

Clean, descriptive URL slugs help search engines understand page content and improve click-through rates. URLs like '/best-coffee-makers' rank better than '/page?id=12345'.

Does it handle non-English text?

Yes! The generator handles Unicode text, transliterating accented and non-Latin characters to their closest ASCII representations for URL compatibility.
