What is Slug Generator?

A slug generator converts any text into a clean, URL-friendly slug. Slugs are the URL-safe versions of titles used in web addresses — like 'my-blog-post-title' instead of 'My Blog Post Title!'.

Our Slug Generator handles spaces, special characters, accented letters, and Unicode text, converting them into lowercase, hyphen-separated URL slugs that are SEO-friendly and readable.

Every web developer, blogger, and CMS user needs clean URL slugs. They improve SEO, look professional, and make URLs easier to share and remember.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a URL-friendly slug from text?

Paste your text (article title, product name, etc.) into the input area and the tool instantly converts it to a clean URL slug. It lowercases text, replaces spaces with hyphens, and removes special characters.

What is a URL slug and why is it important for SEO?

A URL slug is the human-readable part of a URL after the domain (e.g., /my-blog-post). Clean, descriptive slugs improve SEO by telling search engines what the page is about, increase click-through rates, and make URLs shareable and memorable.

Does the slug generator handle special characters?

Yes! The tool removes or transliterates special characters, accents, and non-ASCII characters. It also removes common stop words, collapses multiple hyphens, and trims leading/trailing hyphens for clean, SEO-optimized slugs.
