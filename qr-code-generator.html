What is QR Code Generator?

A QR code (Quick Response code) is a two-dimensional barcode that can store URLs, text, contact information, WiFi credentials, and more. Our QR Code Generator creates high-quality QR codes instantly in your browser.

QR codes are everywhere — from restaurant menus to business cards, product packaging to event tickets. Creating your own QR code is now as simple as typing in your content and clicking a button.

Frequently Asked Questions

What can I encode in a QR code?

You can encode URLs, plain text, email addresses, phone numbers, WiFi credentials, vCards (contact info), and more. URLs are the most common use case.

What size should my QR code be?

For print, ensure the QR code is at least 2cm × 2cm. For scanning from a distance, make it larger. Our generator creates high-resolution images suitable for most uses.

Do QR codes expire?

Static QR codes (like the ones generated here) never expire. They encode data directly, so as long as the QR code image exists and is scannable, it will work forever.

What is error correction in QR codes?

Error correction allows QR codes to be read even if partially damaged or obscured. Higher error correction levels (like H) can survive up to 30% damage but make the QR code denser.
