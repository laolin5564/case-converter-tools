What is QR Code Generator?

A QR code (Quick Response code) is a two-dimensional barcode that can store URLs, text, contact information, WiFi credentials, and more. Our QR Code Generator creates high-quality QR codes instantly in your browser.

QR codes are everywhere — from restaurant menus to business cards, product packaging to event tickets. Creating your own QR code is now as simple as typing in your content and clicking a button.

Our generator creates standard QR codes that work with any QR code scanner. Download your QR code as a PNG image and use it anywhere — in print, on screens, or embedded in documents.

How to Use QR Code Generator

  1. Enter the URL, text, or data you want to encode
  2. The QR code generates automatically as you type
  3. Customize size and error correction level if needed
  4. Download the QR code as a PNG image for use in your projects

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I generate a QR code from text or URL?

Enter your text, URL, or any data into the input area and the QR code is generated instantly. You can then download the QR code as a PNG image for printing, sharing, or embedding in documents and websites.

Can I customize the QR code size and color?

The tool generates standard black-and-white QR codes that are universally scannable. For best results, maintain high contrast between the QR code and background. The generated QR code can be resized without losing scannability.

What can I encode in a QR code?

QR codes can encode URLs, plain text, email addresses, phone numbers, WiFi credentials, vCard contacts, SMS messages, and any other text-based data. URL encoding is the most common use for marketing materials and business cards.
