What is Word Counter?

A word counter is an essential online tool that instantly counts the number of words in any text you provide. Whether you're writing an essay, blog post, or social media update, knowing your exact word count is crucial for meeting requirements and guidelines.

Our free Word Counter tool goes beyond simple counting — it provides detailed statistics including reading time, speaking time, and character counts. It's perfect for students checking essay lengths, bloggers optimizing content, and professionals preparing presentations.

All processing happens directly in your browser, meaning your text is never uploaded to any server. This ensures complete privacy while delivering instant, accurate results.

How to Use Word Counter

  1. Paste or type your text into the input area above
  2. Word count and statistics update automatically in real-time
  3. View detailed metrics including words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs
  4. Use the reading time and speaking time estimates for content planning

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the word counter calculate word count?

Our word counter splits text by whitespace and punctuation boundaries, counting each distinct word. It handles multiple spaces, tabs, and line breaks correctly to give you an accurate count.

Does the word counter work with different languages?

Yes! The word counter works with English and most languages that use space-separated words. For languages like Chinese or Japanese that don't use spaces, character count may be more useful.

Can I count words in a PDF or Word document?

Currently, you need to copy and paste the text from your document into the tool. Simply select all text (Ctrl+A), copy it (Ctrl+C), and paste it into the input area.

Is there a word limit for the counter?

There's no hard limit. The tool processes text of any length, though very large documents (100,000+ words) may take a moment on older devices.
