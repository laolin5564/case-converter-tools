What is Character Counter?

A character counter is a must-have tool for anyone who needs to track the exact number of characters in their text. Social media platforms like Twitter (280 characters), SMS messages (160 characters), and meta descriptions (155 characters) all have strict character limits.

Our free Character Counter provides a detailed breakdown of your text, including total characters, characters without spaces, letters, digits, and special characters. It updates in real-time as you type, making it easy to stay within any character limit.

Whether you're crafting the perfect tweet, writing an SMS, or optimizing SEO meta tags, this tool gives you instant, accurate character counts right in your browser.

How to Use Character Counter

Frequently Asked Questions

Do spaces count as characters?

Yes, spaces are counted in the total character count. We also show a separate 'characters without spaces' count for platforms that don't count spaces.

What counts as a special character?

Special characters include punctuation marks (!@#$%^&*), symbols, and any character that isn't a letter, digit, or space.

Does it count Unicode and emoji characters?

Yes! The character counter handles Unicode characters and emojis correctly. Note that some emojis may count as multiple characters due to Unicode encoding.

Can I use this for Twitter character counting?

Absolutely! Twitter's 280-character limit counts all characters including spaces. Simply paste your tweet text and check the total character count.
