What is Character Counter?

A character counter is a must-have tool for anyone who needs to track the exact number of characters in their text. Social media platforms like Twitter (280 characters), SMS messages (160 characters), and meta descriptions (155 characters) all have strict character limits.

Our free Character Counter provides a detailed breakdown of your text, including total characters, characters without spaces, letters, digits, and special characters. It updates in real-time as you type, making it easy to stay within any character limit.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I count characters in my text?

Paste or type your text into the input area and the character counter instantly shows the total count. It displays both characters with spaces and characters without spaces, plus word count and other useful metrics.

What is the difference between character count with and without spaces?

Character count with spaces includes every character including blank spaces. Character count without spaces excludes all whitespace. Social media platforms like Twitter count characters with spaces, while some academic requirements may specify without spaces.

Can I use this to check social media character limits?

Absolutely! Use our character counter to check your text against Twitter/X (280 characters), Instagram bio (150 characters), LinkedIn posts (3,000 characters), meta descriptions (155-160 characters), and other platform limits.
