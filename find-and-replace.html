Find and Replace Online Free

Find and replace text in bulk with support for case sensitivity

What is Find and Replace?

Our Find and Replace tool lets you search for specific text patterns and replace them with new text across your entire document. It's like the find-and-replace feature in text editors, but works right in your browser with no software needed.

The tool supports case-sensitive and case-insensitive matching, making it easy to do precise bulk text replacements. Whether you're cleaning up data, updating terminology, or reformatting text, this tool handles it instantly.

Process large texts without limits — all replacement happens locally in your browser. Perfect for batch editing, data cleaning, and text transformation tasks.

How to Use Find and Replace

  1. Paste your text into the input area
  2. Enter the text you want to find in the search field
  3. Enter the replacement text
  4. Choose case sensitivity and click Replace to see results

Frequently Asked Questions

Does it support regular expressions?

The tool supports basic text matching with optional case sensitivity. For complex pattern matching, check our Regex Tester tool which provides full regular expression support.

Can I replace text in multiple files?

This tool works with text pasted into the browser. For multi-file replacement, you'd paste each file's content separately or use a desktop text editor.

How do I replace with nothing (delete text)?

Simply leave the replacement field empty. This effectively deletes all occurrences of the search text from your document.

Does it show how many replacements were made?

Yes, the tool shows the count of replacements made, so you can verify the operation worked as expected.
