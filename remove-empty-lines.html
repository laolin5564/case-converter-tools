Frequently Asked Questions

How do I remove blank lines from text?

Paste your text with unwanted blank lines into the input area and click the button. The tool instantly removes all empty lines (lines with no content or only whitespace) and gives you clean, compact text.

Does it remove lines with only spaces or tabs?

Yes! The tool removes both completely empty lines and lines that contain only whitespace characters (spaces, tabs). This ensures your output is truly clean with no hidden blank lines.

When would I need to remove empty lines?

Common use cases include cleaning copied text from PDFs or websites, formatting code, cleaning data files, preparing text for import into databases or spreadsheets, and tidying up any text with excessive blank lines.
